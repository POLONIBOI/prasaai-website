What We Provide

PRASA ai is a WhatsApp-based assistant that helps Cape Town Metrorail commuters with:

  • Train schedule information
  • Delay alerts and service updates
  • Crowd reports from fellow commuters
  • Fare information

All information is delivered through WhatsApp for your convenience.

Accuracy of Information

Our schedule data is sourced from publicly available GTFS (General Transit Feed Specification) feeds. We do our best to keep information accurate and up to date, but schedules may not always reflect real-time changes, cancellations, or disruptions.

We cannot guarantee 100% accuracy. Train services are subject to change without notice by the operators. Always allow extra travel time and check platform announcements where possible.

Crowd Reports

Crowd reports are user-generated — they come from fellow commuters sharing their experience in real time. We aggregate these reports to give you a general picture of how busy trains and stations are.

However, we do not independently verify individual reports. Crowd conditions can change quickly, and reports may not always be accurate or current. Use them as a helpful guide, not a guarantee.
